

# STRYKE Premium Landing Page

A high-end, cinematic single-page website for elite boxing wearable technology, featuring a dark technical aesthetic with cherry red accents.

---

## Brand Foundation

**Color Scheme**: Deep matte black (#050505) background with cherry red (#cc0000) accents used sparingly for maximum impact

**Typography**: Inter font family with heavy weights for powerful headers

**Logo**: Your lightning bolt 'S' logo will be incorporated into the navigation

---

## Page Sections

### 1. Navigation Bar
A fixed, blurred (backdrop-filter) navigation bar that stays at the top while scrolling. Includes the STRYKE logo with lightning bolt, minimal nav links, and a glowing "Get Started" button.

### 2. Hero Section
A full-viewport cinematic hero featuring:
- **Headline**: "MASTER YOUR IMPACT." in bold, heavy typography
- **Subheadline**: Compelling copy about elite boxing wearable tech
- **CTA Button**: Cherry red "Get Started" button with a subtle glow effect
- Animated entrance effects as the page loads

### 3. Product Showcase
A glassmorphism card display highlighting the STRYKE device with:
- High-end abstract placeholder visuals representing the wearable
- Key tech specs: High-frequency sensors, AI-driven insights
- Frosted glass effect cards on the dark background
- Scroll-triggered reveal animations

### 4. The Science of the Strike
An interactive data visualization section featuring:
- **Punch Velocity Chart**: Line chart with red data visualization
- **Force Metrics**: Area chart showing impact power
- **Accuracy Tracking**: Progress-style accuracy metrics
- Dark-themed charts using Recharts with animated data lines
- All charts reveal on scroll with smooth animations

### 5. Feature Grid
A 3-column grid layout showcasing core capabilities:
- **Real-time Analysis**: Instant feedback on every round with lightning icon
- **Fighter Fingerprint**: AI-driven style identification with fingerprint iconography
- **Pro-Level Recovery**: Heart rate and load monitoring with health metrics

Each card features subtle hover effects and scroll-reveal animations.

### 6. Final Call to Action
A bold, high-contrast conversion section with:
- Compelling headline driving action
- Email signup form for waitlist/early access
- The form will be styled but functional (will connect to backend when you're ready)
- Cherry red accent elements for visual emphasis

---

## Technical Implementation

**Animations**: Framer Motion for smooth scroll-reveal effects, hover states, and entrance animations throughout the page

**Charts**: Dark-themed Recharts with cherry red data lines and subtle animations

**Design Effects**: Glassmorphism cards, backdrop blur navigation, subtle glow effects on CTAs

**Responsive**: Fully responsive design that maintains the premium feel on mobile devices

---

## What You'll Get

A single, polished landing page that:
- Conveys elite athletic performance and cutting-edge technology
- Creates urgency and excitement around the STRYKE product
- Captures leads through the email signup form
- Establishes a strong, memorable brand presence
- Is ready to connect to a backend for email collection when needed
